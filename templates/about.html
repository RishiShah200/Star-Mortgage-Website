 About Us

Star Mortgage is a team of experts working together for over 15 years serving many customers to make their dream come true. Located in Jamesburg, New Jersey, we are committed to making your mortgage and refinancing experience exceptional.

We know that each customer has their own specific needs, so we strive to make sure those needs are met using our vast selection of products, investment tools, mortgages, and top quality service and focused individual attention.

Modern technology makes it much easier and more productive to help your refinancing needs. Through our website, you can submit an on-line, secure loan application or apply for a home loan. You can also evaluate your different financing options using our interactive calculators to help suit your needs.
